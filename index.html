Venyou learns what you like without asking too much.
Just install it and be surprised: places will suddenly
decide which music or videos to play based on your preferences,
modulate lighting based on your tastes and much more.


Venyou Hub enables bars, venues and businesses
to dinamically change and provide new kinds of experiences based
on the preferences and tastes of people inside.


The Hub allows automated management of light switches
and smart devices.
